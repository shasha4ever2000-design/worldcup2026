1. Who we are

This site (shasha4ever2000-design.github.io/worldcup2026) is a free, open-source fan companion for the FIFA World Cup 2026. It is not affiliated with FIFA. It is hosted on GitHub Pages and built by Ahmed Hussein. Contact: shasha4ever2000@gmail.com

2. What we store in your browser (localStorage)

The site uses your browser's local storage (a small space on your device) to remember:

  • Your language preference (English or Arabic)
  • Your theme preference (light or dark)
  • Your time zone
  • Your favourite teams and match predictions
  • Your name (only if you typed it in the league or feedback form)
  • Reminders you've set for upcoming matches
  • Friends you've added to your Pick'em league
  • Your cookie consent choice

This data never leaves your device. It's not sent to any server. You can clear it any time from your browser settings.

3. Analytics (Google Analytics 4)

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how many people visit the site, which countries they come from, and which features are used. GA4 may set cookies and collect: page views, language, screen size, approximate location (country/city), referring website, and device type. We do not collect names, emails, or any directly identifying information through analytics.

You can opt out by declining the cookie banner, by using a browser with tracking protection (Firefox, Brave, Safari), or by installing the official Google Analytics opt-out add-on.

4. External services we call

  • flagcdn.com — for team flag images
  • api.open-meteo.com — for match-day weather (sends only the stadium city's coordinates)
  • thesportsdb.com — for live score updates
  • api.rss2json.com — to fetch BBC Sport headlines for the news ticker
  • fonts.googleapis.com / googletagmanager.com — for Google Analytics

These third parties have their own privacy policies. We don't send them your personal data.

5. Feedback form

If you submit a rating or comment via the feedback form, your input is saved locally in your browser. If the site owner has configured a Web3Forms key, your comment is also sent to the owner's email. You may submit anonymously by leaving the name field blank.

6. Pick'em league share links

When you share a Pick'em link with friends, your name and your match predictions are encoded into the URL. Anyone with the link can see them. The data is not stored on any server — it lives only in the URL itself.

7. Advertising

This site may show Google AdSense ads in the future. If/when ads are enabled, Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. You can opt out via Google Ad Settings.

8. Your rights (GDPR/UK GDPR/CCPA)

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your data. Since all personal data lives only in your browser, you can clear it any time via your browser's "clear site data" settings. For analytics data, use the GA opt-out linked above.

9. Children

The site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be noted at the top. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
